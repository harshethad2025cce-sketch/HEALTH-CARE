Diabetes

A chronic condition that affects how your body turns food into energy.

  • Maintain a balanced diet with low sugar.
  • Exercise at least 30 minutes a day.
  • Monitor blood sugar regularly.
  • Avoid smoking and limit alcohol.

Asthma

A condition that affects the airways, causing breathing difficulties.

  • Avoid allergens, smoke, and dust.
  • Use prescribed inhalers properly.
  • Stay hydrated and exercise gently.
  • Seek medical help for severe attacks.

COVID-19

A contagious respiratory illness caused by a coronavirus.

  • Wash hands regularly and wear masks.
  • Avoid crowded areas.
  • Eat immunity-boosting foods.
  • Stay home if you feel unwell.

Mental Health

Mental wellness affects how we think, feel, and act in daily life.

  • Talk to someone you trust.
  • Take breaks and relax often.
  • Get enough sleep and stay active.
  • Avoid negative self-talk.
